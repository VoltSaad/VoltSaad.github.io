About Me

I, Muhammad Saad Mansoor, am a dedicated mechanical engineer with a robust background in enhancing efficiency and improving workflows within the Mechanical Estimation Department at AESOM Private Limited and HVAC internship at Orient Electronics. My experience includes preparing over 500 schedules for mechanical, electrical, and plumbing estimates, mastering PlanSwift software for HVAC systems, and providing critical support for plumbing takeoff counts. I have successfully led projects focused on creating and upgrading electric vehicles with multiple Brushless DC (BLDC) motors, demonstrating my ability to apply engineering knowledge to practical challenges. Passionate about continuous learning and professional growth, I am committed to contributing to innovative solutions in the field of mechanical engineering.
